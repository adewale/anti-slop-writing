# Failure: Generic importance

## Original

```txt
This underscores the importance of durable execution in modern software systems.
```

## Why it failed

The sentence claims significance without showing what durable execution changes. The verb `underscores` makes the line feel conclusive while hiding the mechanism.

## Mechanism

Inflated importance replaces concrete behavior.

## Better rewrite

```txt
The workflow can fail on step 4, retry only that step, and keep the previous outputs.
```

## Rule added or changed

Check every claim of importance against evidence. Replace importance with the mechanism that earns it.
