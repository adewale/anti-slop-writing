## Assessment

Things are better in coverage and traceability. The original scores did not improve because they were already at ceiling, but the suite now tests two gaps the old suite could not see:

1. false positives, where the skill might over-flag earned compression or valid technical language;
2. rewrite quality, where the skill must produce a concrete replacement rather than only naming the slop tell.

The remaining gap is observed trigger-rate testing in actual clients: Pi, Claude Code, Codex, and OpenCode.
