The three surfaces operate on the same event stream at different points in its life. Webhook Sink captures events as they arrive from upstream and writes them to durable storage. Replay Console reads from that storage and re-executes any selected range against the current handler code, which is how you reproduce a bug after fixing it. Timeline View shows the captured and replayed events in the order they were received, so a stuck replay is visible as a gap rather than as a silent failure.
